The Chanteloube Stupas

They are comprised of the 8 types of stupas of the Tibetan tradition, surrouding the original stupa built in 1994.

Their purpose is to protect against war, famine and natural disasters and to promote peace and harmony in the universe.

  • Discover the 8 types of stupas

1. The Lotus Blossom Stupa of the Buddha's Birth

སྐུ་བལྟམས་མཆོད་རྟེན

2. The Enlightenment Stupa

བྱང་ཆུབ་མཆོད་རྟེན

3. The Stupa of Turning the Wheel of Dharma

ཆོ་འཁོར་མཆོད་རྟེན

4. The Great Miracle Stupa

ཆོ་འཕྲུལ་གྱི་མཆོད་རྟེན

5. The Stupa of the Descent from Tushita Heaven

ལྷ་བབ་མཆོད་རྟེན

6. The Stupa of Reconciliation

དབྱེན་བསྡུམ་མཆོད་རྟེན

7. The All-Victorious Stupa

རྣམ་རྒྱལ་མཆོད་རྟེན

8. The Parinirvana Stupa

མྱང་འདས་མཆོད་རྟེན
