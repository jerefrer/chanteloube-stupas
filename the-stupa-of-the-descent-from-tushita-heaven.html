5. The Stupa of the Descent from Tushita Heaven

ལྷ་བབ་མཆོད་རྟེན་

Lhapap chorten

At 42 years of age, Buddha spent a summer retreat in the Tuṣita Heaven where his mother had taken rebirth. In order to repay her kindness he taught the dharma to her rebirth. Local inhabitants built a stupa in Sankassa in order to commemorate this event.

This type of stupa is characterized by having a central projection at each side containing a triple ladder or steps.
