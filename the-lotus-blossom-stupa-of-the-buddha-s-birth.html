1. The Lotus Blossom Stupa of the Buddha's Birth

སྐུ་བལྟམས་མཆོད་རྟེན

Kutam chorten

The Lotus Blossom Stupa symbolizes the birth of Siddhartha, the man who would later be known as the Buddha.

He was born in the Lumbini Garden at Kapilavastu in Northern India, in the sixth century BCE.

The steps of the stupa are round and decorated with lotus petals.
