6. The Stupa of Reconciliation

དབྱེན་བསྡུམ་མཆོད་རྟེན་

Yendum chorten

This stupa commemorates the Buddha's resolution of a dispute among the sangha. A stupa in this design was built in the kingdom of Magadha, where the reconciliation occurred.

It has four octagonal steps with equal sides.
